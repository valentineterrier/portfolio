Project Information

Poster design for the Tulip Festival in Morges, Switzerland.

The concept is inspired by the floral arrangements in the Parc de l'Indépendance, interpreted from an aerial perspective. The tulips are simplified into colourful graphic shapes that reflect the different flower beds while creating a dynamic and rhythmic composition

The art direction is based on a bright, spring-inspired colour palette, reflecting the warm and festive atmosphere of the event. The typography creates a clear hierarchy of information, with the event title and dates taking centre stage, while secondary information remains discreet to maintain readability and the overall balance of the composition.
